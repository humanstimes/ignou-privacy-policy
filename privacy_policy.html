1. Introduction

Ignou (“the App”) is an educational mobile application developed by AmitonMission. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information while you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, phone number, email, address, or account details.

a. Non-Personal Data (Automatically Collected)

Non-personal information may be collected by third-party services such as:

  • Device model & operating system
  • Anonymous app usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers (by AdMob)

b. Push Notifications (FCM)

Ignou uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for sending notifications. FCM may generate a device token to deliver notifications, but we do not store or access this token.

Learn more: Firebase Privacy

3. Features in the App

a. Website Browsing (WebView)

The app allows users to browse educational websites using an in-app WebView. We do not track, log, or collect browsing history.

External websites may have their own privacy policies.

b. Downloading PDFs

The app allows users to download PDF files directly to their device. These files are stored locally and we do not track or access any downloaded content.

c. Sharing PDFs

The app allows users to share PDF files using their device’s share options. Sharing is done locally and is initiated only by the user. We do not collect, upload, or store shared files.

h3>d. Educational Content (Supabase)

We use Supabase only to fetch read-only educational content. No personal user information is collected or uploaded.

More info: Supabase Privacy

e. Advertisements (Google AdMob)

AdMob is used to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization.

Learn more: AdMob Policy

4. Data Security

All data handled by third-party services (AdMob, Firebase, Supabase) is transferred using secure HTTPS connections.

Since we do not store personal data, there is no risk of user identity exposure from our side.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app can be used by all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. User Controls

  • You can disable notifications at any time in device settings.
  • You can disable personalized ads from your Google account or device settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as required. Any changes will appear on this page with a new effective date.
